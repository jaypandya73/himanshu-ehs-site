About Me

I began my career in industrial safety after witnessing a preventable workplace accident that impacted several colleagues. This experience drove me to pursue a lifelong commitment to workplace safety and environmental protection. Over the past 25+ years, I've worked with organizations ranging from small businesses to Fortune 500 companies across manufacturing, healthcare, construction, and chemical processing industries.

My approach combines rigorous technical expertise with practical implementation strategies that work in real-world environments. I believe that effective safety programs must balance regulatory compliance with creating a genuine culture of safety awareness at all organizational levels.
