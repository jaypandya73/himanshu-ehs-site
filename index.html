About Me

I am Himanshu Pandya, a seasoned Environmental Health and Safety professional with over 34 years of rich industrial experience. I retired as Head of HSE from Bayer CropScience Ltd, a renowned German Chemical Multinational Company, where I implemented comprehensive safety protocols and environmental compliance measures across operations. My expertise spans safety audits, regulatory compliance, hazard identification, risk assessment, and developing sustainable EHS management systems. Throughout my career, I've successfully reduced workplace incidents, improved environmental performance, and cultivated strong safety cultures. Now working as a freelance EHS Consultant and Trainer, I bring my extensive multinational experience to help organizations enhance their safety performance, comply with regulations, and protect both their workforce and the environment.

Professional Philosophy

"Safety is not just compliance with regulations—it's creating an environment where every person instinctively makes decisions that protect themselves and others. My mission is to help organizations build that culture from the ground up."

Professional EHS Services

Risk Assessment

Management Systems
